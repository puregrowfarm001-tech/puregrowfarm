About Pure Grow Farm

Pure Grow Farm is a family-run mushroom farm focused on sustainable oyster mushroom cultivation. We grow on locally sourced substrate, avoid chemical treatments and pack produce with care to deliver maximum freshness.

  • Organic substrate and eco-friendly methods
  • Fresh mushroom, dry mushroom and value-added mushroom products
  • Student internship and farmer training farm visits
